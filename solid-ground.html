Why This Exists

There is so much for women with partners. Nothing for us.

There are courses for mums in business. Programs for couples building something together. Coaches who assume you have someone at home to sanity-check ideas with, split the bills while you figure it out, or talk you down at 10pm when nothing is working.

If you're doing this without that safety net - by choice or coincidence - you already know the advice doesn't fit. The risk is different. The decisions land differently. And the loneliness of it is something most business content doesn't even acknowledge.

"I built this because it didn't exist when I needed it. I'm still in the middle of it. Five years in, third version of the business, still building. That's not a gap in credibility - that's the credential."

Solid Ground is what Jess wishes had been there in 2020. The framework. The real talk. The things nobody puts in the curriculum. Built specifically for women with no partner income as a safety net, and no one to hand things off to - solo by choice or coincidence.
