Insurance basics
This is an overview only — not financial advice. A broker can help you find the right cover for your specific type of work.
Professional indemnity
Covers claims that your advice or service caused harm. Most relevant for coaches, practitioners, healers, consultants. Some professional associations require it before you can practice — check yours.
Public liability
Covers you if a client is injured or property is damaged during your work. Essential if you see clients in person — your space, their space, a studio, events.
Income protection
Covers your income if you're too sick or injured to work. Sole traders have no sick leave. This is the one most people skip — and the one they most regret skipping.
Most service-based businesses pay a few hundred dollars a year. Get at least two or three quotes. Check your professional association for preferred providers before going direct.
