I grew up in a house where children were seen and not heard - and that applied to our emotions too.

The message I received, over and over, was that feelings were something to be contained. So I got very good at containing them. I bottled everything up until it would explode - everywhere, over the smallest things. The straw that broke the camel's back. That was my emotional baseline for years.

Long term, it had a real impact. On my behaviour, my choices, my mental health. I didn't have the language for what was happening, and I certainly didn't have the tools.

It wasn't until early 2022, when I started putting this process together, that things actually started to change.

Emotion Reset is what I wish I'd had.

What Is It

21 days back to yourself

Emotion Reset is a guided 21-day process designed to help you deeply reconnect with both your emotional and physical body. Over three weeks, you'll work through prompts, questions, and self-reflection that guide you gently through what's been building - so you can process it, release it, and return to a place of peace, balance, and grounded emotional wellbeing.

This isn't a quick fix or a mindset reframe. It's a genuine reconnection process - one that meets you exactly where you are and moves at your pace.
