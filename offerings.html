Do I need to believe in psychics for this to work?
Not at all. An open mind is all that's needed. Many of my clients come in sceptical and leave surprised. You don't have to believe - just be willing to explore.
What if I cry or get emotional?
Then you're in the right place. This is a safe space for exactly that. Emotions are information - there's nothing to be embarrassed about here.
How long are sessions?
Session length varies depending on the offering. Check the booking page for specific details on each session type, or reach out and I'll help you find the right fit.
Can I have a session if I'm not spiritual?
Absolutely. You don't need a spiritual background or practice. I work with women from all walks of life - religious, spiritual, agnostic, and everything in between.
What's the difference between Clarity & Healing and Expansion?
Clarity & Healing is for anyone seeking insight and support. Expansion is specifically designed for practitioners and healers who want to go deeper in their own inner work alongside their professional development.

If your body responded reading any of this, that's your sign. Book a Clarity & Healing session - we'll work out the rest from there.
