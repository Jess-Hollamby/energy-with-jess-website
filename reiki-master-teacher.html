Prerequisites

Is this the right next step?

You are ready if…

  • You hold Reiki II (or higher) and have been practising regularly
  • You have been working with energy for at least 1-2 years since your last attunement
  • Reiki is a genuine part of your daily life - not just something you do occasionally
  • You feel drawn to the depth of Master level - not just the qualification
  • You want to teach (Master/Teacher track) and feel ready to hold space for others in this work

You might want to wait if…

  • Your Reiki II attunement was recent and you have not had much time to integrate it
  • You are completing Reiki II soon - finish that first and give it time
  • You are drawn to the certificate more than the practice - this level asks more of you than a course

Not sure? Get in touch before joining the waitlist.

Program Outline

What you will cover

Each session is approximately 5 hours, delivered fortnightly via Zoom.

Reiki Master — 4 Sessions

1

Foundation

Reiki I and II review, what shifts at Master level, Shielding and Protection in depth - why this matters more at Master level, techniques and guided practice.

2

Symbols and Attunement

Usui lineage and history, the Dai Ko Myo Master symbol, Raku, symbol integration practice, receiving the Master attunement, and what to expect in the 21-day integration.

3

TCM and the 5 Elements

An introduction to Traditional Chinese Medicine as a complementary lens for Reiki practice. The 5 Elements, Yin/Yang, the Three Treasures, and how to read energy through the TCM framework in your sessions.

4

Integration and Closing

Ethics at Master level, business basics for practitioners, a full practice session, and certificate presentation.

Master/Teacher Add-on — 3 Additional Sessions

5

Teaching Reiki I

Teaching philosophy, all four Reiki I attunement protocols, how to structure and run a Reiki I class, and setting up to teach online.

6

Teaching Reiki II

Both Reiki II attunement protocols, how to structure a Reiki II class, and distant attunements using the empty chair method.

7

Teaching Reiki III and Graduation

The Reiki III Master attunement protocol, curriculum design using the STICK method, business foundations for Reiki teachers, and certificate presentation.
