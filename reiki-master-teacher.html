Two Pathways

Choose the path that calls to you

Both pathways begin with the Master attunement. Where they differ is in what you're able to do next.

M
Pathway One

Reiki Master

For those who want to work with Reiki at the deepest level for themselves and in their treatments with others - without moving into teaching. You'll receive the Master attunement and the Master symbol, significantly deepening your capacity to channel and work with Reiki energy.

This pathway is complete in itself. Many experienced practitioners choose it to elevate their existing work without taking on a teaching role.

Prerequisites: Reiki Level 1 & 2
Coming Late 2026
T
Pathway Two

Reiki Master & Teacher

For those called to teach. You'll receive the Master attunement, learn the Master symbol, and gain everything you need to attune students at all three levels of Reiki. You become a Reiki lineage holder in your own right - with the ability to pass on this practice to others.

This is a significant step. It asks not just for skill, but for the readiness to hold space for others in their own energetic awakening.

Prerequisites: Reiki Level 1 & 2
