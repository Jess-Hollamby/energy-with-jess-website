My Story

I know what it feels like to carry everyone else's energy

I'm Jess - a Psychic, Reiki Master/Teacher, and Empath based in Adelaide, South Australia. I built Energy with Jess for women who've spent their lives being told they're too sensitive, too much, or too emotional.

I know that feeling intimately. For a long time I thought my sensitivity was a flaw - something to manage, hide, or apologise for. It took years of doing the inner work to realise it was actually my greatest gift.

The moment I stopped trying to fix myself and started learning to work with my energy instead of against it, everything shifted.

That shift is what I want for you. Whether you're navigating grief, burnout, relationship patterns, or just a deep sense that something isn't quite right - I'm here to help you find your way back to yourself.

I work across multiple modalities including psychic readings, Reiki, tarot, and one-on-one mentoring. My approach is deliberately slow, gentle, and personal - because real change doesn't come from force, it comes from flow.

My Approach

Slow. Gentle. Entirely yours.

I don't believe in cookie-cutter healing. Every woman who comes to me is treated as an individual - because your path is your own, and what works for someone else may not be what you need.

I encourage experimentation over prescription. I'll share tools, practices, and perspectives - but you're always the one who decides what resonates and what doesn't.

And you don't need to keep working with me forever for me to be your biggest cheerleader. Some women come for one session and walk away with everything they needed. Others stay for the long haul. Both are completely right.

  • Non-judgmental - you can say anything here
  • No spiritual gatekeeping - wherever you are is the right place to start
  • Practical as well as energetic - real life, real results
  • No pressure to commit to more than you're ready for
  • Available in-person (Adelaide & Port Elliot) and online worldwide
