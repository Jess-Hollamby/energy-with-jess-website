What This Is

The place where everything begins to shift

Reiki is a Japanese energy healing system that uses hand placements on and above the body to promote relaxation and wellbeing - affecting a person's energy levels, stress, and health in ways that are both gentle and profound.

It is an amazing self-help tool that aids in balancing the mind, body, and spirit. And Level 1 is where your practice begins.

This is your entry point. Your first attunement. The beginning of a relationship with energy that, once opened, stays with you for life.

Over 2 x 5-hour days, you'll move through the full foundation of Reiki - from its history and philosophy through to giving hands-on treatments to yourself and others. You'll receive four attunements, which open you to channel Reiki energy. And you'll leave with a practice you can use every single day.

Many students say the same thing after Level 1 - they didn't realise how disconnected they'd been from their own body and energy until they started finding their way back.
