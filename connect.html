Good to Know

A few things before you book

You don't need to be prepared

Seriously. You don't need a list of questions, a clear intention, or any particular frame of mind. Show up however you are. The session will do the rest.

Everything stays between us

What's shared in session stays in session. This is a confidential space and I take that seriously. You can say anything here.

There's no pressure to continue

One session can shift something on its own. I'll never push you toward more sessions than you need or are ready for.

It's okay to be a sceptic

An open mind is all you need. Many of my clients arrive uncertain and leave with something they didn't expect. Curiosity is enough.

I'm not a replacement for mental health support

My work is energetic, intuitive, and spiritual in nature. If you're experiencing acute mental health challenges, please also seek support from a qualified mental health professional. I'm a complement to that care, not a replacement for it. If you're unsure whether working with me is appropriate for where you're at, send me an email and we'll have an honest conversation.
