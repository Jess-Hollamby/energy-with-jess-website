What is Reiki?

A gentle, powerful way to work with your own energy

Reiki is a Japanese energy healing practice that works with the body's natural energy field to promote deep relaxation, emotional release, and balance. It's gentle, non-invasive, and available to anyone - regardless of background or belief.

When you learn Reiki, you receive an attunement - an energetic activation that opens you to channel Reiki energy. You don't create the energy; you become a conduit for it. And that changes things.

Learning Reiki gave me something I didn't expect - a way to come back to myself whenever I needed it.

Whether you want to use it for your own daily practice, support your family and friends, or build a professional healing practice - learning Reiki is one of the most practical, accessible, and genuinely powerful things you can do.

What people learn Reiki for

  • Daily self-care and personal healing
  • Supporting family, friends, and pets
  • Building a professional healing practice
  • Deepening an existing spiritual practice
  • Adding to a complementary health offering
  • Pure curiosity - and that's enough

Where do you want to begin?

Each level builds on the last - but you don't have to commit to all three upfront. Start where feels right.

I
The Beginning

Reiki Level 1

Your entry point into energy healing. Level 1 is about learning to work with Reiki energy for yourself - and for those closest to you. You'll receive your first attunement, learn the history and principles of Reiki, and begin a hands-on practice you can use every single day.

This is where the shift begins. Many students find Level 1 alone changes how they relate to their own body, emotions, and energy.

  • First Reiki attunement
  • History and principles of Reiki
  • Hands-on self-healing practice
  • Working with others at a foundational level
  • Certificate of completion

II
Going Deeper

Reiki Level 2

Level 2 expands your practice significantly. You'll learn the Reiki symbols - tools that allow you to focus energy more precisely, work across distance, and go deeper in your healing sessions. This is the level most practitioners use professionally.

Distance healing becomes available at this level, meaning you can offer Reiki to anyone, anywhere in the world - without being in the same room.

  • Second Reiki attunement
  • The three core Reiki symbols
  • Distance healing techniques
  • Working with emotional and mental patterns
  • Certificate of completion
Learn More Book Level 2
III
Coming Late 2026

Reiki Master & Teacher

The highest level of Reiki training. At Master level you receive the Master attunement, learn the Master symbol, and - if you choose to teach - gain the knowledge and ability to attune others. You become a Reiki lineage holder in your own right.

This isn't just a qualification. It's a deepening of your relationship with energy itself - and the beginning of a lifelong practice of teaching, sharing, and holding space.

  • Master attunement
  • The Master symbol
  • How to attune students at all levels
  • Teaching frameworks and practice
  • Certificate of Master/Teacher completion
