Emotion Reset

"I grew up in a house where children were seen and not heard - and that applied to our emotions too. I bottled everything up until it exploded over the smallest things. That straw-that-broke-the-camel's-back feeling? I lived there for years. It wasn't until early 2022, when I started putting this process together, that things finally started to shift."

Emotion Reset is a 21-day process designed to help you deeply reconnect with both your emotional and physical body. Over three weeks, you'll work through prompts, questions, and self-reflection that gently guide you back to yourself - releasing what's been building, restoring the connection between how you feel and how you function, and returning to a place of peace, balance, and grounded emotional wellbeing.

Once you purchase, you'll find a link inside the PDF to join the private Facebook and Telegram groups - a supportive space to share your responses, ask questions, and move through this process with real-time company alongside you.
