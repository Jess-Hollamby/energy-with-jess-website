Workflow 1
Lead Capture and Email Sequence
Applies to: any lead who submits your Google Form (freebie opt-in or enquiry)
↓
Lead trigger

Lead submits Google Form

They fill in their name, email, and how they found you. Their details are automatically added to your Google Sheet.

A
Automated

Email 1 - Confirmation (immediate)

Sends within seconds of form submission. Delivers the freebie or confirms their enquiry. Sets the tone for the relationship.

This email is auto-sent by Apps Script. Write it in your voice - warm, not transactional.
A
Automated

Email 2 - Follow-up (Day 3)

Sent automatically 3 days after signup. A gentle check-in - did they get what they needed? Opens the door to a conversation.

A
Automated

Email 3 - Value and soft offer (Day 7)

Sent automatically 7 days after signup. Provides value and includes a soft call to action - book a call, learn more, or take the next step.

M
Manual - you review and send

Email 4 - Personal check-in (Day 14)

A draft is created automatically in Gmail for you to review. You personalise it based on what you know about this lead, then send.

This one is personal - it should feel like it's written just for them, because it is.
M
Manual - you review and send

Email 5 - Final touchpoint (Day 30)

A draft is created automatically in Gmail. Final nudge before the sequence ends. After this, the lead stays in your sheet - you can follow up manually any time.

After the sequence ends: The lead stays in your Google Sheet. Update their pipeline stage as the relationship develops. You can always follow up manually - the system keeps their record.
Workflow 2
Direct Booking
Applies to: leads or clients who book directly via your booking page (not via the freebie form)
↓
Lead trigger

Lead books via booking page

Books and pays directly via your booking system. No form submission needed.

A
Automated

Booking confirmation sent by booking system

Your booking platform sends the confirmation with session details and joining link automatically.

M
Manual - add to sheet

Add lead to Google Sheet

Add them manually to your CRM sheet so they're tracked alongside all other leads and clients. Set their pipeline stage to Booked or Client.

↓
Session

Session takes place

Update their pipeline stage after the session. Add any notes directly in the Google Sheet.
