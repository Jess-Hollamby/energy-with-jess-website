Common Questions

What people usually ask

What is Reiki healing?

Reiki is a gentle energy healing practice that works with your body's own energy field to release tension, clear blocks, and restore a sense of calm and balance. Sessions are deeply relaxing and non-invasive - you remain fully clothed throughout.

Do I need to believe in it for it to work?

No. Reiki works on a physical and energetic level regardless of what you believe going in. Many people who are sceptical have their most profound sessions. An open mind is enough.

Is online Reiki as effective as in-person?

Yes. Distance Reiki works with energy, not proximity - so location doesn't limit the session. Jess works with clients across Australia and internationally online with the same results as in-person.

How many sessions will I need?

One session can shift a lot. Some people come once when they need it, others book regularly as part of how they look after themselves. There's no prescription - you'll know what feels right after your first session.

What's the difference between Reiki and Intuitive Healing?

Reiki follows a structured approach using the traditional Usui system. Intuitive Healing is more fluid - Jess draws on Reiki alongside other modalities depending on what you need. Both are deeply relaxing and work with your energy field.
