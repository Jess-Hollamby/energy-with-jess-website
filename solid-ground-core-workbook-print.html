Module Three
The Tools That Do the Job
Free, functional, and nothing you don't need yet.
Tick each one off as it's done. Nothing moves forward until this list is complete.
Minimum viable tech stack
Professional email address set up (Google Workspace free tier)
Google Drive folder created for business files
ABN registered — abr.gov.au, free, 5 minutes. Must be done before your first invoice.
Canva free account set up
Booking link live and tested (Calendly free tier or Google Form)
Payment method set up — Stripe or PayPal (pick one)
Test payment sent and received successfully
Separate bank account open for business income
Business money tracker set up (resource attached to this module)
Invoice template saved and ready to send
Insurance checked — professional indemnity at minimum before your first paid client; public liability if you see clients in person
Professional indemnity covers claims that your advice or service caused harm. Public liability covers you if a client is injured while you're working. Most service-based businesses can get covered for a few hundred dollars a year — worth getting quotes before you take on paying clients.
