A Signature Program

Your darkness is not the enemy.

There are parts of your soul that have never seen the light - not because they are wrong, but because the world told you to put them away. And so you did. You locked them into corners, built walls around them, learned to perform a version of yourself that felt safer than the whole truth.

But energy doesn't disappear. It waits.

The shadow holds everything you couldn't afford to be. Your power. Your rage. Your grief. Your wildness. The pieces of your soul that got lost along the way - tucked behind shame, buried under expectations, hidden in the places you've been too afraid to look.

This program is a descent. A sacred, guided descent into those places - to meet what's there, to honour it, and to bring it home. Because the integration of your darkness is not destruction. It is the most profound expansion you will ever experience.

Who This Is For

The shadow has been calling you.

This is for you
  • You can feel there is more of you - parts that have never been fully seen or lived.
  • You've done the work on the surface and something deeper is still pulling at you.
  • You feel called toward your shadow, your edges, the parts you've been told to hide - and you're finally ready to turn toward them.
  • There are things you've noticed that you've never said out loud - presences, impulses, something seen, heard, or felt that no one else seemed to notice. Something at the edges you've sensed but never known quite what to do with.
  • You understand that true expansion lives on the other side of what you've been avoiding.
  • You're ready to stop performing wholeness and start actually living it.
  • You hold a Reiki Level 2 certification (or higher) - this is a prerequisite for entry. The energetic work in this program builds on that foundation week by week.
This isn't for you
  • You're looking for a spiritual bypassing experience - light and love only, no shadow.
  • You don't yet hold a Reiki Level 2 certification - this foundation needs to be in place before entering the container.
  • You're not ready to sit with what's uncomfortable - and that's okay. Timing is everything.
  • You're in active crisis without professional support structures in place. This work goes very deep.
